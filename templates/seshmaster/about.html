ABOUT US

Here at SESHMASTER we aim to provide an easy to use service to help our users
find their ideal Nightclub location in Glasgow.

Whether you're looking for a chill night out with mates or a rave to remember
here at SESHMASTER we have you covered.

Search for your ideal Nightclub based on price range, music genre, location,
user rating, or name. Whatever you're looking for, we're sure to have it here.

When you do find the club you're looking for feel free to leave a review of
it with us and we'll display it underneath the club's page for everyone to see.
If you feel like it you could even upload pictures of the club that you took
on your night out to show everyone the club from your perspective.


For Nightclub owners we offer the ability to make a page for any and all of
your clubs. Just simply get in contact with us and we'll give you
owner privileges on the site.
